Embedded System And IoT Workshop

Workshop Outcome

Internet of Things is the inter-networking of physical devices, vehicles, buildings, and other items, embedded with electronics, software, sensors, actuators, and network connectivity that enable these objects to collect and exchange data. By learning the inter-connection and data exchange, Students can create their own IoT Networks with connected devices and can access it from anyware in the world. This course will provide the participants knowledge and experience on the fast growing field of IoT.
