INDUSTRIAL AUTOMATION Workshop

Workshop Outcome

Industrial automation systems are systems used to control and monitor a process, machine or device in a computerized manner that usually fulfills repetitive functions or tasks. They are intended to operate automatically in order to reduce and improve human work in the industry. The workshop aims in providing quality stuffs needed for engineers for automating industy related works using bots and embedded systems.
